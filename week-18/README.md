# Week 18: Project 4 -- Speed Build

**Theme:** Build and ship a complete project in 5 days. Prove you can move fast.

Everything you've learned over 17 weeks comes together here. You're going to build and ship a complete, production-quality application in a single week. Not a toy. Not a demo. A real product with auth, a database, testing, monitoring, and a live deployment.

This is the speed test. Can you research, plan, build, test, and ship under time pressure? The answer is yes -- because you have 17 weeks of foundations and a proven workflow. Speed doesn't come from cutting corners. It comes from knowing exactly what to do and doing it decisively.

---

## Learning Objectives

1. **Execute under time pressure** -- make fast decisions without sacrificing quality.
2. **Apply time-boxing** -- know when to stop polishing and move to the next phase.
3. **Leverage your full toolkit efficiently** -- pick the right tools immediately, no experimentation.
4. **Ship a complete product in 5 days** -- research through deployment in one week.

## What You Ship

A **complete, deployed application** with authentication, database, core features, basic testing, monitoring, and documentation. Built from scratch in 5 days.

## Time Commitment

~15-20 hours across the week. This is an intensive week by design.

## Prerequisites

- **Weeks 1-17 completed.** You have the full skill set and multiple shipped projects.
- A GitHub account and Vercel account.
- Neon Postgres access.

## How to Approach This Week

1. **Read guide.md** before Day 1. Understand the framework so you don't waste time figuring out the process.
2. **Pick your project quickly.** Spend time on Day 1 planning, not deciding.
3. **Follow the daily structure.** Each day has a clear goal. Hit it.
4. **Ship by end of Day 5.** No extensions. The constraint is the point.

This week is proof that you can move at professional speed. Companies ship features in a week. Freelancers deliver projects in a week. Startup founders build MVPs in a week. Now you can too.
